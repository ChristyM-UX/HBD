Process
How I typically approach a project from start to ship.

1. Frame the problem. Align with the team on goals, constraints, and who we’re designing for.

2. Explore and map. User journeys, flows, and quick sketches to understand the space.

3. Prototype. Move into wireframes and interactive prototypes to make ideas testable.

4. Test and iterate. Get feedback from users and the team, then refine the details.

5. Hand-off with care. Clear documentation, design specs, and support during implementation.
